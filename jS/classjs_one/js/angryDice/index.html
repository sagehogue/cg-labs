Angry Dice!

The Rules


  • If you roll two Angry Dice, you will experience PURE ANGER!
    You will start again from round 1.
  • You may hold one die at a time, preventing it from being rolled.
  • You cannot hold a 6.

Objectives

  • Round 1:
    • You must roll a one and a two.
  • Round 2:
    • You must roll a three and a four.
  • Round 3:
    • You must roll a five and a six to win.
